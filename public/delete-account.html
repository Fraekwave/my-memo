Delete your INA Done account

You can delete your INA Done account either inside the app or from the web. Deleting the account permanently removes your synced tasks, tabs, profile settings, and trash.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the account deletion flow below.
  2. Sign in if needed.
  3. Open Account & Privacy.
  4. Choose Delete account and confirm the action.
Open account deletion

If you are using the Android app and this page opens inside the app shell, open the same URL in a desktop browser or a browser-only support host once that host is configured for Google Play compliance.
